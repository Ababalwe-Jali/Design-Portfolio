How I Think About Design

My approach to creating products that balance user needs, business goals, and meaningful digital experiences.

My Philosophy

Great design isn't about adding more. It's about removing friction.

I believe exceptional products emerge when user needs, business goals, and technology align naturally. My role as a designer is not simply to create beautiful interfaces but to uncover problems, simplify complexity, and build experiences that feel intuitive from the first interaction. Every decision should have a purpose. Every interaction should create clarity.

System Thinking

Design System

A scalable collection of design principles, components, typography, colors, and interaction patterns that ensure consistency, accessibility, and efficiency across digital products.

Color

Blue is the only interactive color — no secondary brand hues. Neutrals carry the visual weight.

Typography

Display / 600

Heading / 500

Body text at 15px regular — clear, readable, no decoration needed.

Eyebrow / Uppercase

Buttons

Spacing & Radius
